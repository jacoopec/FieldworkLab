If you truly have no labels: use unsupervised methods only to find structure

clustering: split customers into behavior groups
anomaly detection: find unusual customers
but these do not directly give a true binary target


choose a cutoff date
define who was inactive at that date
check who reactivated in the following window
assign target = 1/0
train a binary classifier on features built before the cutoff

last_purchase_before_cutoff <= cutoff - inactivity_window  -> eligible inactive client
any purchase in future outcome window                      -> target = 1
no purchase in future outcome window                       -> target = 0
